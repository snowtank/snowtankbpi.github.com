What is Banana Pi D1?

The Banana Pi D1 is a small open-source development board with an on-board HD video camera capable of capturing 720p videos at 30fps.
The D1 comes with all you need to set-up a web connected video camera including a USB OTG WiFi support, external battery connector, microphone, and more. This is the perfect board that can provide the hardware platform for many fun projects.
Banana Pi is an open platform device, it is for anyone who wants to play and build with developer technology instead of simply using consumer technology. Backed by our community, starting a project and building servers is fun and rewarding. We welcome all companies, DIYers, and tech loving people within our community! Together, we can make a difference, we can discover our passions, inspire others, and build a practical project.

D1 Specifications

CPU 400MHz ARM926EJ 32Bits RISC Core
GPU ARM Mali-400 MP2 with Open GL ES 2.0/1.1
Memory 64MB DDR2
Storage Support 16MB SPI-FLASH and SD card(up to 32GB)
Onboard Network USB WIFI (OPTION):WiFi module can be switch between the AP and the SLAVE mode,
easy to configure the WiFi module; BPI-D1 support WPS mode
Video In It achieves H.264 hardware encoding at 1280x720p_30fps.
32 gb SD card can store 120 hours of video data
Video Out
Audio Out
Audio In On board microphone
Power Source 5V DC via Micro USB
Power Consumption WiFi ON 5v - 300mA
Power Consumption Recording 5v - 200mA
Power Consumption Estimate 24 hours continous recording with 10000mAh power pack
Battery Port 3.7v Li-ion socket
USB Ports 1x USB OTG
Buttons Reset: next to Micro USb
Power: next to reset
Boot(optional): behind HDMI connector
GPIO 1UART/2GPIO； 2PWM/2GPIO； I2C； AudioLineIN ；HPAudioOutL ；HPAudioOutR
LED Power key
OS Linux3.4.35, Kernel Operation system that makes secondary development possible
Dimensions 38mm x 38mm
Weight 10g
